New Game

Welcome to Maurice's Great Adventure, a game about a rat that has to find his cat owner. You take on the role of Maurice the rat and must journey to find his lost cat owner, Zeus. There are plenty of friends and enemies along the way, as well as plenty of room for just exploring. You may also want to help as many people as possible along the way, as rats can use as much kindness with humans as they can get. Remember, lost cats can get very hungry!

Game Controls

UP Arrow -- Move Character Up
LEFT Arrow -- Move Character Left
RIGHT Arrow -- Move Character Right
DOWN Arrow -- Move Character Down
e -- View Character's Current Equipment
i -- Open Character Inventory
t -- Talk To NPC(Non-Player Character, also opens shops)
g -- Grab Item Under Character's Feet
c -- View Character's Status
